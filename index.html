About Me

Hi! My name is Peng Yuhan, or GuYang17 on the Internet.

My main hobby is programming. I've absolutely forgotten how many years past since I was hooked on it. When I was in middle school, I loved playing Minecraft, a famous sandbox game. It's undoubtedly that Minecraft has a large mods nature, and someday I made a decision to make a Minecraft mod by myself. Java is a fun programming language, though I have forgotten it's hello world code. My first mod is about food, adding various foods like dumplings and fried egg to Minecraft. Such a funny thing! Day after day I exited Minecraft, but my love for programming did not die. I got a book, The C Programming Language (K&R), from my friend, which opened my eyes to the full world of computer. So I learned C, a language full of unfathomable pointers. One day I tried the lengendary code editor - Vim, then I fell in love with making TUI programs. A TUI painting program is a example. Now, I am still learning and making. Golang and Rust is my next goal. Hope to become a good programming and open source lover!

As a high school student, I am always trying my best to get good grades, like many other student in China. English is one of my best and favorite subject, and my life is full of this language, in music, films, also in computer. I specialize in Chinese, Math, English, Physics, Chemistry and Tech.

Speaking of daily life, it is just very simple. I hardly ever do any sports. I sometimes play Chinese Chess with friends. I love listening music, especially rock and pop. I sometimes lie on the sofa and watch films, but after entering high school, it is definitely impossible.

I will always try, because I know - The only way to fail is to give up.
